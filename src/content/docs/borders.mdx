<TextBlock>

Maintain the <a href="https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/CSS/border" target="_blank">border</a> and <a href="https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/CSS/border" target="_blank">border radius</a> styles globally and on specific selectors.

</TextBlock>

<Divider id="borderUtilities" />

<TextBlock>

## Border Utilities

Add a border to any element with Natura11y's border utilities. Include a <a href="#1">breakpoint modifier</a> to apply a border at a specific breakpoint (Figure 1).

</TextBlock>

<FigureBlock
    figureNumber="1"
    visualExample={(

                    <div class="grid__colspan-2 padding-3 subtle-fill border">
                        <p class="margin-bottom-1">
                            Border (all sides)
                        </p>
                        <p><code>.border</code></p>
                    </div>
                    <div class="padding-3 subtle-fill border-top">

                            Border (top side only)
                        </p>
                        <p><code>.border-top</code></p>
                    </div>
                    <div class="padding-3 subtle-fill border-right">
                        <p class="margin-bottom-1">
                            Border (right side only)
                        </p>
                        <p><code>.border-right</code></p>
                    </div>
                    <div class="padding-3 subtle-fill border-bottom">

    codeExample={(`

        <!-- Breakpoint Specific (e.g., medium breakpoint) --> \n
        <div class="border--md">...</div>
        <div class="border-top--md">...</div>
        <div class="border-right--md">...</div>
        <div class="border-bottom--md">...</div>
        <div class="border-left--md">...</div>
    `)}
