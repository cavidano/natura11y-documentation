## Banner Headline

The `.banner-headline` utility is named after the large headline in a newspaper that stretches across the top of the front page. It is useful for styling a header larger (or visually different) than the typical `<h1>` heading.

</TextBlock>

<FigureBlock
    figureNumber="1"
    visualExample={(
        <p className="banner-headline">
            Banner Headline
        </p>
    )}
    codeExample={(`
        <h1 class="banner-headline">
            Banner Headline
        </h1>
    `)}
/>

<TextBlock>

## Section Headings

Natura11y provides six levels of section headings, `<h1>` through `<h6>`. Styles for each level are consistent for a tag header (e.g. `<h1>`) and a utility class header (e.g. `.h1`).

</TextBlock>

<FigureBlock
    figureNumber="2"
    visualExample={(
        <Fragment>
            <p class="h1">Header 1</p>
            <p class="h2">Header 2</p>
            <p class="h3">Header 3</p>
            <p class="h4">Header 4</p>
            <p class="h5">Header 5</p>
            <p class="h6">Header 6</p>
        </Fragment>
    )}
    codeExample={(`
        <h1>Header 1</h1>
        <p class="h1">Header 1</p> \n
        <h2>Header 2</h2>
        <p class="h2">Header 2</p> \n
        <h3>Header 3</h3>
        <p class="h3">Header 3</p> \n
        <h4>Header 4</h4>
        <p class="h4">Header 4</p> \n
        <h5>Header 5</h5>
        <p class="h5">Header 5</p> \n
        <h6>Header 6</h6>
        <p class="h6">Header 6</p>
    `)}
/>
