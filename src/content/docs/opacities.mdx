Change the <a href="https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/CSS/opacity" target="_blank">opacity</a> property of an element. While opacity utilities work well with <a href="/docs/backdrops">backdrops</a>, you can apply them to any element you wish. 

</TextBlock>

<TextBlock>

## Opacity Utilities

The opacity utilities (Figure 1) range from ***10*** to ***90*** in increments of 10. For example, `.opacity-50` applies a 50% opacity to its selector. 

</TextBlock>
